JEAN-LUC PICKACHU
(Pokémon's mascot since 1996)
Pikachu is a yellow mouse Pokémon from the fictional Pokémon world that was created by Satoshi Tajiri. Pikachu is the mascot of the Pokémon franchise, and one of the main characters of the Pokémon anime series. It has also appeared in most Pokémon video games and merchandise. In the video game Pokémon Yellow, it is the player's starter Pokémon.

In the anime series, Pikachu is Ash's first Pokémon. Pikachu is an electric-type Pokémon, which attacks using electricity from two red pouches on its cheeks.
